## The Myth

**Purpose**: State the misconception clearly and explain why people believe it
**Target length**: ~150 words

Present the myth as many people understand it — state it fairly and without condescension. Explain why this belief is so widespread: Is it intuitive? Was it once true? Does it come from a trusted but outdated source? Acknowledge that believing the myth is understandable. This builds trust with readers who currently hold this belief and makes them more receptive to the correction that follows.

## Reality Check

**Purpose**: Reveal what is actually true and why it differs from the myth
**Target length**: ~200 words

Transition from the myth to reality with a clear pivot. State the truth plainly and explain the key differences from the myth. Walk through the logic: why does the reality work differently than people assume? Use a concrete example or scenario to illustrate the gap between myth and reality. Make the explanation accessible — avoid jargon and use analogies if the concept is technical.

## The Evidence

**Purpose**: Back up the reality with credible data and sources
**Target length**: ~200 words

Present 2-3 pieces of supporting evidence. These could be research studies, industry data, expert opinions, case studies, or historical examples. For each piece of evidence, explain what it shows and why it is credible. Address potential objections: "But what about...?" Anticipate the reader's skepticism and meet it with solid reasoning. If the evidence is nuanced or context-dependent, acknowledge that honestly rather than oversimplifying.

## The Takeaway

**Purpose**: Give the reader a practical action or mindset shift based on the truth
**Target length**: ~150 words

Now that the reader knows the truth, what should they do differently? Provide a specific, actionable recommendation. Explain how applying this understanding will benefit them in their work, decisions, or daily life. Close with an encouragement to question other widely-held assumptions — the skill of critical thinking is the real gift of this article.

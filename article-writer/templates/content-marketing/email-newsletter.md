## Featured Content

**Purpose**: Deliver the main value of the newsletter in an engaging, scannable format
**Target length**: ~400 words

Open with a personal, conversational greeting that makes the reader feel like they are receiving a message from a trusted friend or advisor, not a brand. Transition into the featured content with a hook — a question, observation, or timely reference.

Present the main topic in digestible chunks. Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max), bold key phrases, and bullet points where appropriate. Emails are scanned, not read word-by-word, so front-load the most valuable insight.

Include one actionable takeaway the reader can implement immediately. If referencing external content (a blog post, resource, or tool), provide a clear link with context about why it is worth clicking. Close the featured section with a transition to the quick links below.

## Quick Links

**Purpose**: Curate 3-5 valuable resources, articles, or updates for the reader
**Target length**: ~200 words

Present 3-5 curated links, each with:

- A descriptive title or label.
- One sentence explaining why this link is valuable or what the reader will gain.
- The link itself (or placeholder).

Mix content types — include a mix of your own content, industry news, tools, and community highlights. Keep descriptions punchy and benefit-focused. End the newsletter with a brief sign-off that reinforces your brand voice and invites replies or engagement.

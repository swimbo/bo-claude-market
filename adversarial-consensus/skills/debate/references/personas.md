# Persona Catalog

This file contains the full curated persona pool organized by domain. Each domain includes keyword triggers used to match plan content to relevant personas.

When selecting personas, analyze the plan for domain keywords and pick 3-5 personas that provide **maximally diverse perspectives** on the plan's subject matter. Always include at least one persona outside the plan's primary domain to surface blind spots.

## Persona Selection Algorithm

When selecting personas for a debate:

1. **Extract keywords** from the plan (section titles, technology mentions, domain terms)
2. **Score each domain** by counting keyword matches
3. **Select top 2-3 domains** by score
4. **Pick 1-2 personas per top domain** — choose those whose "Perspective Bias" is most relevant to the plan's specific challenges
5. **Add 1 contrarian persona** from a non-matching domain to surface blind spots:
   - Security persona for non-security plans
   - Cost/FinOps persona for feature-heavy plans
   - UX/Product persona for infrastructure plans
   - DevOps/SRE persona for application-level plans
6. **Total: 3-5 personas** (3 for simple plans, 5 for complex multi-phase plans)

### Complexity Heuristic

- **Simple** (3 personas): Single-phase, single-domain, <500 words
- **Medium** (4 personas): Multi-phase OR multi-domain, 500-2000 words
- **Complex** (5 personas): Multi-phase AND multi-domain, >2000 words, or architecture-level decisions
